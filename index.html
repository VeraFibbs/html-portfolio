Vikings

This is my favourite movie.It's a Historical drama;Action War. Ragnar Lothbrok, a legendary Norse hero,is a mere farmer who rises up to become a fearless warrior and commander of the Viking tribes with the support of his equally ferocious family.

Troy:Fall of a City

An epic retelling of the 10-year siege of Troy. Told from the perspective of the Trojan Family,this series delves into the rich history of the Trojan war and Love.

The Last Kingdom
